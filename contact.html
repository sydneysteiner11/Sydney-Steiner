ABOUT THE DESIGNER

SYDNEY STEINER

Sydney Steiner is a Graphic Designer based in Minneapolis, Minnesota. In addition to her work in graphic design, she also have a strong passion for photography, film and illustration. With a strong background in Adobe Creative Cloud software, she provides a versatile and detail oriented outlook to her creative work. From forming compelling visual identities to the creation of engaging print and digital compositions, Sydney guides her designs to best communicate the clients vision.

MISSION

Art that combines a touch of vintage and a glismpe towards the future. My work explores a feminine outlook while intergrating sterile and disarranged content to provoke a dialogue between fragility and emotional chaos. There is a quiet tension within my work, one created from built up beauty and disruption. Visual story telling is a key component, where layering imagery and contrasting elements build narratives that blur the line between past and futuristic content.
